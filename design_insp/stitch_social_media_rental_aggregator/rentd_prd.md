# Rentd Product Requirements

## Overview
Rentd aggregates rental housing listings from unstructured social media posts and transforms them into clean, structured, and searchable property listings.

## Key Features
- **Data Ingestion:** Standardizes raw social media posts (descriptions, images, contact info).
- **Structured Listings:** Extracted fields include:
    - Location
    - Rent range
    - BHK (Bedrooms)
    - Furnishing status
    - Amenities (AC, Washing Machine, Fridge, WiFi, Parking, etc.)
    - Deposit requirements
    - Tenant preferences
    - Availability dates
- **Discovery & Search:** Filter by budget, property type, furnishing, and amenities.
- **Lead Generation:** Direct contact options (call/message) for owners/agents.
- **Confidence Indicators:** Reflects completeness/availability of extracted info.

## User Persona
Renters looking for homes across various localities in a city, wanting to avoid manually parsing noisy social media data.
